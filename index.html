Simple Instructions

The process is easy:
  1. Download the IRS f19909 reporting document
  2. Fill out the required information (your name, etc.)
  3. Email the form to the IRS at eoclass@irs.gov

Below is the proper form already prefilled with all The Heritage Foundation's full information. All you have to do is put in your name and email the form to the IRS.
